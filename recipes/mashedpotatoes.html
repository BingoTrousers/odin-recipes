Mashed Potatoes

Description

You like mashed potatoes, huh? Well here's a recipe for mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 3 large russet potatoes, peeled and halved lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Put potatoes in a pot. Cover with cold, salted water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer until tender and can be pierced with a fork, 20 to 25 minutes.
  2. Drain potatoes then return them to the pot. Turn heat to high and allow potatoes to dry for about 30 seconds. Turn off heat.
  3. Mash potatoes with a potato masher a couple of times, then add in milk and butter and continue mashing until smooth and fluffy.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper; whisk until evenly distributed, about 15 seconds.
  5. Serve.
